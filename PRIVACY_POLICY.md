## What We Collect

| Data                 | Encryption                              | Retention              |
| -------------------- | --------------------------------------- | ---------------------- |
| Phone number         | AES-256-GCM                             | Until account deletion |
| Telegram session key | Access-controlled server infrastructure | Until account deletion |
| Message text         | AES-256-GCM                             | 30 days                |
| Message metadata     | AES-256-GCM                             | 30 days                |
| Media files          | None — plaintext storage                | 24 hours               |
| Activity logs        | Server-level                            | 30 days                |

We do not store your Telegram password, 2FA code, or payment details.

---

## Media Files Warning

Photos, videos, and voice messages are stored without encryption for 24 hours, then permanently deleted. This means that in the event of a server breach or a lawful request from US authorities, media files would be accessible in plaintext during that window.

By activating a subscription tier that includes media monitoring, you explicitly accept this risk.

## Deleting Your Data

Send `/delete_account` to the bot and confirm.

**Immediately:** session revoked, phone number removed, media files deleted, activity logs deleted.
**Within 24 hours:** all message content and metadata permanently deleted.

Deletion is irreversible. No data is recoverable after this process completes.

## Withdrawing Consent

Where processing is based on your consent, you may withdraw that consent
at any time by contacting botb86792@gmail.com and specifying which
processing activity you wish to withdraw consent for.

The following applies depending on which consent you withdraw:

**Cross-border transfer.** If you withdraw consent to the transfer of
your data to servers in the United States, we will be unable to continue
providing the Service, as all data processing occurs on US-based
infrastructure. In this case, your account will be deleted in accordance
with the procedure described in the Deleting Your Data section.

**Message content and media monitoring.** If you withdraw consent to
the processing of message content and media files, the applicable
subscription tier will be deactivated immediately and no further
monitoring will occur. Previously captured data will be deleted within
24 hours of your request.

Withdrawal of consent does not affect the lawfulness of processing
carried out before the date of withdrawal. Processing based on
contractual necessity — your phone number and session key — continues
for as long as your account remains active, as these are required for
the Service to function.
